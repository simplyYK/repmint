# RepMint Experiment Launch Checklist

## Before Launch

- Confirm landing page headline matches the current promise: AI camera trainer for goal-based workouts.
- Confirm CTAs are visible above the fold: `Join Pilot` and `Take Survey`.
- Add a real survey link if using Google Forms, Typeform, or Tally.
- Add basic analytics or manual tracking for visits and CTA clicks.
- Create a simple spreadsheet from `RESULTS_TRACKER.csv`.
- Prepare recruiting messages for each channel.
- Prepare the concierge pilot consent script.
- Choose 2-3 pilot workout templates.
- Assign one teammate to record results.

## Suggested Tracking Setup

Minimum viable:

- Track page visitors from hosting analytics.
- Track email pilot requests.
- Track survey completions.
- Add `?source=` links per channel.

Example source links:

- `/ ?source=classmates`
- `/ ?source=gym`
- `/ ?source=instagram`
- `/ ?source=reddit`

Better:

- Add Plausible, Vercel Analytics, or Google Analytics.
- Track events for `Join Pilot`, `Take Survey`, and `See How It Works`.
- Use one survey question for source attribution.

## Recruiting Messages

### Short Message

We are testing RepMint, an AI camera trainer for solo workouts. It creates goal-based plans, counts reps, tracks time under tension, and gives short set feedback through your phone camera. If you train alone, could you review the page and join the pilot or take the 3-minute survey?

### University Gym Message

Do you train alone at the gym or at home? We are validating RepMint, an AI camera training companion that gives goal-based workouts, rep counting, TUT/tempo tracking, and set review. We are looking for quick feedback and a few pilot testers this week.

### Pilot Invite

We are running a 20-minute RepMint pilot. You will pick a goal, do a short bodyweight workout, and receive a RepMint-style set review. It is product research, not medical guidance. Interested?
