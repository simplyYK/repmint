# RepMint Survey

Use this as a Google Form, Typeform, Tally, or classroom survey. Estimated completion time: 3 minutes.

## Survey Intro

We are testing RepMint, an AI camera trainer for solo workouts. RepMint would create goal-based training plans, guide sets through your phone or laptop camera, count reps, track time under tension, summarize sets, and recommend what to focus on next.

This is for product research only. RepMint is not medical guidance. Please do not share sensitive health details.

## Scoring Guide

Qualified respondent:

- Works out at least once per week.
- Trains alone at least sometimes.
- Gives at least one pain point related to planning, form, progress, reps, tempo, or consistency.

Strong lead:

- Rates goal-based plans 4 or 5.
- Rates camera feedback 4 or 5.
- Says yes or maybe to pilot.
- Leaves email or asks for follow-up.

Key output:

- Rank features by average score.
- Count top pain points.
- Count camera comfort average.
- Count pilot yes/maybe rate.
- Pull repeated words from open-text answers for landing page copy.
